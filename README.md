## ü•Ω About Glimpse GLB

**Visualize your 3D assets in standalone VR & Mixed Reality.**

Getting 3D models onto a standalone headset can be a friction-heavy process. **Glimpse GLB** removes the barriers. We standardized on the **GLB format** because it is the industry leader for portability‚Äîembedding textures, materials, and animations into a single, efficient file.

**Privacy First:** No clouds, no accounts, no tracking. Your models stay on your device.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Installation
Glimpse GLB is available directly on the **Meta Horizon Store**. You can install it on your Quest without sideloading.

[![Get it on Meta Quest](https://img.shields.io/badge/Meta%20Quest-Available%20on%20Store-blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=meta)](https://www.meta.com/nl-nl/experiences/glimpse-glb/25467602739525995/)

> **[Click here to download Glimpse GLB](https://www.meta.com/nl-nl/experiences/glimpse-glb/25467602739525995/)**

### 2. Loading Models
Glimpse GLB looks for files in your headset's `Download` folder. Here is the easiest way to get them there:

* **Google Drive / Cloud:** Open the Quest Browser, log into your Drive, and download the file.
* **Sketchfab:** Browse Sketchfab in the Quest Browser and download the `.glb` file.
* **USB Transfer:** Connect your Quest to your PC via USB and drag files into the `Download` folder.
