Why Glimpse GLB?

Before I made this app, I found it difficult to find a 3D model viewer for the Quest 3 and get the models on the headset. I had to make some choices for the easiest workflow.


Why GLB specifically?

I chose GLB exclusively over FBX and OBJ because GLB embeds all textures and materials in the file. Most applications can export GLB, it can have animations, and it supports albedo, metal, gloss, and lightmaps.

Loading Models
Local Files & Preparation

Loading files directly from the internet is possible, but typing long URLs in VR is annoying. Therefore, Glimpse GLB focuses on local files. Here are the easiest ways to transfer them:

  • Google Drive: Open the Quest browser, head to your drive, and save the GLB to your downloads folder.
  • Sketchfab: Find the GLB file you like in the browser, and save the file to downloads.
  • USB Cable: Connect your headset to your computer. Click "Allow access to data" in the headset. Copy your files to the Quest 'Download' folder via your PC explorer.

Environment & AR
Passthrough Mode

Control the atmosphere of your viewing experience. The environment panels enable you to switch the lighting and environment of the model.

You can toggle the Augmented Reality space (Passthrough mode) to see the model in your own room, or switch between a Skybox or Sun & Sky. Use the lighting slider to give you more control over the appearance.

Interaction & Hierarchy
Total Control

Interact fully with your 3D objects. You can move, click, and adjust materials on the fly.

Need to see inside? Show or hide parts in the hierarchy. You can also scale models from tabletop size up to 1:1 scale for a realistic experience.

Key Features

Everything you need to inspect your 3D work on the Meta Quest.

  • User Interface

    Tutorial walkthroughs, toggleable UI panels, and easy teleport/reset functions.

  • Object Interaction

    Move, scale (Tabletop vs 1:1), modify materials, and toggle hierarchy visibility.

  • Environment

    Toggle Augmented Reality (Passthrough), change Skyboxes, time of day, and lighting grids.

  • Easy Loading

    Support for Google Drive, Sketchfab, and USB transfer to local storage.
