Streamline Your 3D Workflow

Getting 3D models onto a standalone headset can be a friction-heavy process. Glimpse removes the barriers by supporting the formats you already use.


Optimized for GLB, Built for Versatility

While we recommend the GLB format for its efficiency and all-in-one portability, Glimpse now supports FBX, OBJ, and STL to ensure your existing assets are always viewable in VR.

  • GLB Standard
  • Multi-Format
  • Fast Loading

Seamless Local
File Management

Glimpse focuses on a "Local First" approach. Simply drop your .glb, .fbx, .obj, or .stl files into your Quest's Downloads folder and you're ready to go.

  • Cloud Downloads: Use the Quest Browser to download assets directly from Drive or Sketchfab.
  • Legacy Support: Bring your classic OBJ and FBX files into VR without conversion.
  • 3D Print Previews: Check your STL files at real-world scale before printing.
