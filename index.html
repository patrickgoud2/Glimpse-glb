Core Capabilities

Built for professionals and enthusiasts alike. Everything you need for efficient 3D inspection.

High-Fidelity

Support for albedo, metalness, roughness, and lightmaps for ultra-realistic rendering directly on your standalone headset.

Animation & Morphs

Experience full control over complex skeletal animations and detailed Morph Targets (Blend Shapes).

Mixed Reality

Toggle Passthrough dynamically to place your 3D objects directly in your living room or office space.

Multi-Format Support

Fully supporting FBX (Rigged), OBJ (Static), and STL (3D Printing) alongside the standard GLB format.
