Privacy Policy

Glimpse GLB for Meta Quest

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

1. Overview

We believe that your privacy is a fundamental right. Glimpse GLB is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. We simply do not collect your data.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, or Meta account details.
  • Your physical location or IP address.
  • Usage statistics, crash reports, or analytics.

3. Local Processing Only

Any data generated during your use of the app stays exclusively on your Meta Quest 3 headset.

  • Spatial Data: If the app uses Passthrough or Mixed Reality features to detect your environment, this data is processed locally by the hardware. We do not have access to your room layout or camera feeds.
  • Save Files: Any progress or settings are saved locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party analytics (such as Unity Analytics, Firebase, or Metaâ€™s tracking SDKs) that would track your behavior across the platform.

5. Permissions

The app may request system permissions (such as Microphone for voice input or Spatial Data for Mixed Reality). These permissions are used solely to enable the features of the app in real-time. We do not record or export any data obtained through these permissions.

6. Changes to This Policy

Because we do not collect any data, a change in this policy would only occur if we added a feature that required data collection. In such a case, we would update this policy and notify users through the app update notes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this "No-Data" policy, please contact us at: info@patrickgoud.com
